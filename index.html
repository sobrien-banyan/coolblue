Reference

This is a picture of a typical split system air conditioning unit. As you can see there is an outdoor unit (the condensing unit) and the indoor unit (the air handler) that are connected with refrigerant piping. Please feel free to use this picture as a reference when booking an appointment and describing your system. There is another popular type of air conditioning system call a package unit. Coil Cleaning

This is an extreme case. The dirty coil in the picture causes air flow restriction can lead to serious damage or system failure. Even slight air flow obstructions will result in lower efficiency and an increase electric bill. Keeping the coil clean and removing any items that are to close to it will ensure the system is running in peak condition. Call us today to have your coil inspected.
